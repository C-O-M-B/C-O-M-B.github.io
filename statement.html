Disclaimer

Costs may vary. All service prices are listed at base cost. Our stylists reserve the right to generate their own fees per hour, and/or per amount of product used.

Automatic Gratuity

We love our staff and we know you do, too! To ensure that they are properly compensated for their time and effort, Comb will be adding a 20% gratuity to all transactions of $200 or more.

Product Return / Exchange Policy

It is our pleasure to provide you with a refund, in the original form or payment, or exchange within seven (7) days of purchase if the product remains unopened, sealed, and unused. If the item has been opened and used, we will offer an exchange for a product that is better suited for you.
All make-up products, hot tools, hair brushes, travel-size or discounted items are non-refundable.
If you would like to test a product prior to commiting to a purchase, please ask and we'll be happy to provide a demonstration of use and answer any questions.
We also provide free samples of our products.

Cancellation Policy

We respectfully request at least 24 hours advanced notice for cancellations. We know how easy it is to forget an appointment you booked months ago, which is why we confirm appointments a day in advance.
With any appointment made, we reserve the right to ask for a credit card to keep on file to secure the appointment.
-Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice are subject to a charge equal to 50% of the reserved service amount.
-Total "NO SHOWS" will be charged 100% of the reserved service amount.
-Appointments made within the 24 hour period must give notice of cancellation at least 4 hours prior to the appointment. Those cancelled with less than 4 hours notice will be subject to a charge equal to 50% of the reserved service amount.
